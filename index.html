Behaviour consultant

Welcome to the world of expert canine training, where understanding, patience, and skill converge to create well-behaved and happy furry companions. With over 10 years of dedicated experience in both Ireland and the UK, Laura Mc Cormac stands as a beacon of excellence in the realm of dog training.

Through countless hours of working with dogs of all breeds, sizes, and temperaments, Laura has honed an unmatched expertise in fostering harmonious relationships between pets and their owners. Whether it's addressing behavioral issues, obedience training, or specialized needs, Laura brings a wealth of knowledge and a compassionate approach to every interaction.

Education

As a testament to their commitment to the welfare of dogs, Laura stays abreast of the latest advancements in animal behavior science and training techniques. Their dedication extends beyond just training dogs; it encompasses educating owners, empowering them with the tools and understanding necessary to nurture a lifelong bond with their furry companions.

Whether you're struggling with obedience challenges, seeking to cultivate better communication with your pet, or simply eager to enhance the quality of life for your beloved canine, Laura is here to guide you every step of the way. Trust in their expertise and let Laura lead you and your furry friend on a journey of growth, learning, and mutual understanding.

Trust and confidentce

Laura specializes in the proven method of positive reinforcement training, where desired behaviors are rewarded with treats, toys, or affection. This approach not only shapes obedience but also fosters a strong bond based on trust and mutual understanding.

By focusing on encouragement and praise, Laura empowers your dog to make positive choices and actively participate in the learning process. With their expertise in positive reinforcement techniques, you'll witness your furry friend's confidence grow as they eagerly respond to training with enthusiasm and joy.
